Drivers are responsible for damages. The area is large with almost no obstacles, and a professional driver is present — crashes are very unlikely.

How many people can join?

No fixed minimum or maximum. We may limit numbers in some cases for safety or quality.

When do I pay?

Payment must be made in advance. Your slot is confirmed after payment.
